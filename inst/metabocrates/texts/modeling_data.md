This table shows the data used for modeling and the response variable. It also includes:

* fitted - predicted probabilities,
* resid - residuals (the difference between observed and fitted values).

For the full model (if fitted):

* hat - diagonal of the hat (projection) matrix,
* cooksd - Cook's distance,
* std.resid - standardized residuals.
